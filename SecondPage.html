Personal Life

I grew up and spent 18 years of my life in Saint Petersburg Florida. I lived with my two parents, Molly and James Camp, and my younger sister Lizzie. I went to the same school for 15 years. I have been a swimmer since I was 10. I became extremely competitive when I got to middle school and started to compete at the varsity level when I was in 7th grade. I won four state championships and was an All American in the 50 and 100 freestyle. While at home, I enjoy spending time at the beach, going to local restaurants, and shopping downtown. I also really enjoy going skiing in different resorts out west. When I am at school, I spend a lot of time with my friends going out, watching movies, and just hanging out.
